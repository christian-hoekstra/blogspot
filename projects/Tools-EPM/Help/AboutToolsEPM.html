About Tools-EPM

Introduction ››

The goal of Tools-EPM library is to provide command-line based utilities that simplify the daily tasks of an technical EPM consultant/engineer. Tools-EPM exposes and extents build-in hidden EPM functionalities. The author aims for full multi-platform compatibility (identical to EPM OS certification), however not all platforms have been tested.


Although Tools-EPM can be considered as [stable], it is still in development. Expect future functionality updates and bug fixes. Please check for updates regularly at christian-hoekstra.blogspot.com.
