Compare two OPatch inventory reports

Comparing two OPatch inventories allows you to detect differenances. This might come in handy in case of a distributed high available environment, where you have two or more idential servers and you want to compare patch levels.

A table is printed to the console that shows only the patches that are different.


D:\> manageOPatch.cmd|sh -cx <SERVERNAME_EPMSystem11R1_lsinv_YYYYmmdd_HHmmss>,<SERVERNAME_EPMSystem11R1_lsinv_YYYYmmdd_HHmmss>



In this example SERVER1 has 22204291, 21909693 and 22106770 installed, and SERVER2 not. SERVER2 however has 21909692, 22106771 and 20717672 installed, and SERVER1 not.
